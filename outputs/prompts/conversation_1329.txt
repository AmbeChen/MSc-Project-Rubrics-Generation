Assistant: Key points for a workshop on informed consent:

1. **Definition**: Emphasize the process of ensuring patients understand the nature and risks of a procedure.
2. **Capacity**: Assess decision-making ability, highlighting mental status and voluntary participation.
3. **Disclosure**: Discuss guidelines for sharing relevant information (risks, benefits, alternatives).
4. **Documentation**: Outline proper forms and notation of patient understanding.
5. **Policy Variations**: Note institutional differences and local legal requirements.
