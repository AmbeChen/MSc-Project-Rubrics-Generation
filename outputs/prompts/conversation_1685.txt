User: need info on measles vaccine schedule
Assistant: The CDC recommends children receive two doses of the measles vaccine: the first between 12-15 months, and the second between 4-6 years of age.
User: what about adults not vaccinated as kids
Assistant: Adults without evidence of immunity should receive at least one dose of the MMR vaccine. In certain situations, two doses may be recommended.
User: are there side effects for meningitis vaccine
Assistant: Yes, common side effects of the meningitis vaccine include soreness at the injection site, mild fever, and fatigue. These side effects are typically mild and resolve on their own.
