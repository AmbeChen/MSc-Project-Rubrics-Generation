User: What in ear infection
Assistant: An ear infection (also known as otitis) is inflammation or infection of the middle or outer parts of the ear, often caused by bacteria or viruses. It can lead to ear pain, discomfort, or temporary hearing changes.
User: Ear infection cause
Assistant: Ear infections can result from a cold, flu, allergies, or anything that leads to fluid buildup behind the eardrum. Children and adults with nasal congestion or sinus issues may be at higher risk.
