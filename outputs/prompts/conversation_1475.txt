User: Cirrhosis advanced stage mein hai, ascites (fluid retention) aur hepatic encephalopathy ke symptoms aa rahe hain.
Assistant: Ji haan, advanced cirrhosis mein ascites hone ka matlab hai ki liver theek se function nahin kar paa raha, jisse paani pet ya tangon mein jama ho sakta hai. Hepatic encephalopathy mein zehan par asar padta hai, kyunki liver toxins ko filter nahin kar paa raha.

**Possible Treatments**:
- **Diuretics** (paani nikalne ke liye), jaise ki Spironolactone ya Furosemide.
- **Lactulose**: Yeh ek syrup hai jo gut mein ammonia ka level kam karta hai.
- **Antibiotics** jaise Rifaximin kabhi-kabhi use hota hai hepatic encephalopathy ke liye.

Lekin dhyaan rahe, medicines ki availability ya brand names alag-alag jagah par different ho sakte hain. Aapko ek specialist se consult karna chahiye, taaki aapke local resources aur specific condition ke hisaab se best advice mil sake.
